Target Audience

Our objective is to strengthen entrepreneurs and micro-enterprises in the city of Monterrey who wish to increase the exposure of their businesses. That they can acquire a commercial identity that allows them to do business with other affiliated micro-enterprises. Our associates have access to the following services: financing, training courses, advertising, meeting rooms, etc.

Personas

Gilberto Cienfuegos

Age: 40
Education: Technician
Ethnicity: Hispanic
Status: Married

Goals:
Gilberto has a wife and two children, he studied a technical degree in computer science, he is a specialist in repair and maintenance of computers, laptops and cell phones. He has a small business at home, but lately, things are not going well, his motivation is family, his wife and children, but the little ones grow up and he worries about their well-being and future. His plan is to relocate his business to an established location, in a shopping mall where he has a better position and thus can get new customers. To achieve this he needs advice and financial support.

Pedro Pablo

Age: 37
Education: Truncated career
Ethnicity: Hispanic
Status: Single

Goals:
Pedro did not finish his university career, he works in his parent's small grocery store, and for a while he has observed that his business is stagnant, not prospering. Pedro is an only child and is concerned about the care of his parents, he needs to cover their needs, as well as his own. For several years he have heard about chambers of commerce, but are unaware of the role of these institutions. When conducting research on the Internet, he discovers the Nuevo León Chamber of Commerce and finds interesting the benefits that are obtained as a member.

Quote:
"Walking in a straight line one can not get very far."
Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

Scenarios

  1. Francisco has not had job stability and is thinking about the possibility of starting a small business. Francisco sees an ad on Facebook about the Chamber of Commerce and visits the page to research support for entrepreneurs and small businesses.
  2. Sebastián and his family come to visit the city and need a guide on hotels, attractions, and restaurants. The page offers some suggestions that cover the needs of tourism.
  3. Arnold is a foreign student and passionate reader, he needs to buy a book but he doesn't know the city. Visit the page to find the locations of the closest bookstores in the city.
  4. A young entrepreneur decides to start a business selling computer hardware, he goes to the page to find out the registration requirements to be part of the chamber's members and thus be able to contact trusted local dealers.
  5. A group of young people want to climb one of the city's mountains over the weekend, Fernanda suggests visiting the page to find out the weather forecast.
