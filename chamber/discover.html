Mirador del Obispado

The Mirador del Obispado (Bishops Lookout) is located at the top of the Cerro del Obispado (Bishops Hill) in the northern city of Monterrey, Mexico. It features the biggest bandera monumental (monumental flag) in Mexico. The hill and the lookout receive their name from the building constructed in the middle of the hill by the end of the 18th century, the Palacio del Obispado (Bishops Palace).
